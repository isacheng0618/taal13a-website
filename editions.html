Why editions

We do not want Dutch learning materials to feel like disposable worksheets. Taal13A Editions turns useful language into objects you can keep, use and live with.

我们不想让荷兰语学习资料只像一张张用完就丢的练习纸。Taal13A Editions 把有用的语言做成可以保存、可以使用、可以进入生活的物件。

From China. Living in the Netherlands.

We look at Dutch language and culture through the eyes of a young Chinese generation making a life in the Netherlands.
